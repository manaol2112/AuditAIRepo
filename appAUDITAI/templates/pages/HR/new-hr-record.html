The Active Employee List is a comprehensive, up-to-date record of all employees and their associated user accounts within an organization's IT systems. It includes information such as employee names, job titles, employee ID numbers, usernames, access permissions, and relevant access credentials.

A New Hire List is a specific subset of employee-related data that is particularly relevant during the onboarding process of new employees. It is closely related to the Active Employee List but is primarily used to manage and track new employees' integration into the organization.

A Termination List, sometimes referred to as an Employee Offboarding List, is a record of employees who are leaving an organization, whether due to resignation, retirement, or termination. It is a crucial component of an organization's HR and IT processes, and its purpose is to facilitate a smooth and secure transition for departing employees.
